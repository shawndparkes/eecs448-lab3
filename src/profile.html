Jon Snow is the son of Rhaegar Targaryen and Lyanna Stark. He was raised by his uncle, Lord Eddard Stark who was originally thought to be his father. He eventually would join the Night's Watch and bring himself to become the Lord Commander of the Night's Watch. However, he would be killed by a number of his own men. After being brought back to life he would hang his murderers and go reclaim back his family's home from the Boltons to become the new King of the North.
